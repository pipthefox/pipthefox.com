avalanche sizes (log-log)

—

Click anywhere on the grid to drop grains. When a cell reaches 4, it topples — sending one grain to each neighbor. The cascade that follows is an avalanche. After enough drops, the system organizes itself to a critical state where avalanches of all sizes occur, following a power law. No tuning required.
